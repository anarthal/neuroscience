Cell structure: eukaryotic
    Have cell wall
    ERGOSTEROL: molecule embedded in fungi plasma membrane, with similar effects to human cholesterol
YEAST: unicellular fungus
    Asexual reproduction through budding: part of the yeast buds out of the original cell to form a new one
MOLD: multicellular fungus that forms a network of cells
    HYPHAE: each of the filaments composing the structure of a mold
        Cells in hyphae may be separated by septa (septate) or not (aseptate)
    MYCELIUM: set of connected hyphae forming a web. The main mass of a fungus
    Asexual or sexual reproduction using spores
DIMORPHIC FUNGI: switch between yeast and mold forms depending on environmental conditions
