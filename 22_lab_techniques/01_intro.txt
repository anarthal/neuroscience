Optimal T = 21ºC (although might go to 25ºC)
HEPA filter: High efficiency particulate air system (filters air)
PPE = Personal Protection Equipment
BSL = Biosafety Level
    BSL-i inherits BSL-i-1 safety measures
    BSL-1: no isolation (limited access), gloves, coats
    BSL-2: moderately dangerous pathogens and animal cells. Cabinets 
        Cabinet air flow from top to bottom
    BSL-3: more serious diseases, inhalation. Coveralls (head and shoes - not really required for BSL-2)
        Cabinet doesn't have direct access, only with gloves
    BSL-4: rare
