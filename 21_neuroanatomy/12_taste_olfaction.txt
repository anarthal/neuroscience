Taste pathway
    1st order neurons in ganglia of CN, depending on taste bud position
        Vagus (X): nodose ganglion
        Glossopharyngeal (IX): petrosal ganglion
        Facial (VII): geniculate ganglion
    Converge in solitary tract
    Synapse in gustatory nucleus/lateral solitary nucleus (medulla oblongata)
    Some fibers to hypothalamus and limbic system
    Other fibers to ipsilateral thalamus (VPM) => Primary gustatory cortex (insula)
